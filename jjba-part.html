Something about JOJO

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki. It was originally serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine from 1987 to 2007, with the first volume released in 1988. The series follows the lives of several generations of protagonists, including Jonathan Joestar, Joseph Joestar, Josuke Higashikata, and Giorno Giovanna, who are all connected by their encounters with supernatural forces and the Stand, a psychic power that manifests as a physical entity.
